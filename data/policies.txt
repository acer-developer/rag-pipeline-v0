Returns: Items may be returned within 30 days of purchase for a full refund, provided they are unused and in original packaging.
Exchanges: Exchanges are accepted within 45 days of purchase with a valid receipt.
Shipping: Standard shipping takes 5-7 business days. Express shipping takes 2 business days.
Shipping Costs: Orders over $50 qualify for free standard shipping. Express shipping is a flat $15.
Warranty: All electronics come with a 1-year limited warranty covering manufacturing defects.
Damaged Items: Report damaged or defective items within 7 days of delivery for a free replacement.
Refund Timing: Refunds are processed within 5-10 business days to the original payment method.
Support: Customer support is available Monday to Friday, 9am to 6pm, by email and phone.
